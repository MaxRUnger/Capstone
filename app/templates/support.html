Common Questions

How do I import grades from a PDF?

Navigate to Upload Grades, select your assignment, upload your PDF or image file, review the extracted grades, and click Import to add them to your gradebook.

How do I reorder learning objectives?

Go to Learning Objectives, then simply drag and drop objectives using the drag handle icon on the left side of each objective card.

Where is my data stored?

All student information and grades are stored in our secure remote database, ensuring your data is safe and accessible across all your devices.
